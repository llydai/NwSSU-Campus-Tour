Step-by-Step Enrollment

1

Visit the Registrar's Office

Submit: Form 138, PSA Birth Certificate, Good Moral Certificate, and 2×2 ID photos.

2

Entrance Examination

Take the University Entrance Examination (UEE). Results posted within 3–5 days via bulletin board or online portal.

3

Assessment & Enrollment

Once accepted, get your assessment form from your college. Choose subjects and schedule with your academic adviser.

4

Payment at the Cashier

Pay tuition and miscellaneous fees. Ask about CHED scholarships and financial assistance programs.

5

Get Your Student ID

Return to the Registrar for ID processing. Required for library access, labs, and all campus services.
